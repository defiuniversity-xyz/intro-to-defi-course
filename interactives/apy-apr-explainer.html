APY vs APR Explainer

Understand the difference between these important terms

APR

Annual Percentage Rate

Simple interest. Does NOT include compounding. What you earn if you never reinvest.

APY

Annual Percentage Yield

Compound interest. INCLUDES compounding effect. What you actually earn if you reinvest.

APR (no compounding)

12%

$1,000 → $1,120

APY (with compounding)

12.75%

$1,000 → $1,127.50

Compounding earns you 0.75% more! That's $7.50 extra on $1,000.

Key Takeaway

APY is always ≥ APR. When comparing DeFi opportunities, make sure you're comparing the same metric! A 10% APY is NOT the same as 10% APR.
